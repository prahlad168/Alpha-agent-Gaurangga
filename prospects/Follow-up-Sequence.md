## 🎯 BEST PRACTICES

```
DO:
✅ Personalize setiap message
✅ Reference previous conversation
✅ Share social proof
✅ Give value first
✅ Be helpful, not pushy
✅ Track everything

DON'T:
❌ Send same message to everyone
❌ Be too salesy
❌ Give up after 1-2 tries
❌ Ignore their responses
❌ Spam
```
