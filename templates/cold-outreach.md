## 🎯 BEST PRACTICES

```
BEST PRACTICES:
- Personalize setiap message
- Fokus pada VALUE, bukan selling
- Use social proof (client results)
- Follow up 3-5x sebelum give up
- Best time to send: Tue-Thu, 9-11 AM
- Use mobile-first approach (WhatsApp)
- Jangan terlalu salesy di awal
- Jangan spam
```
